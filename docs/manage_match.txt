
Steps for offline (past) Match details entry:

1. Enter and finalize Squad for both teams
2. Enter Actual date-time of Kickoff, Halftime, second half start, Final time
3. Enter Events with Minutes: 
	- First half is from 1-20, first addl time should be given as for 20+n, e.g 20+1 for 1st minute of addl time
	- Second half is from 21-40
  - Second half addl time should be given as 40+n, e.g 40+2 for second minute of addl time
